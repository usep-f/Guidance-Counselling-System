Terms of Use

Basic rules for using the Guidance System responsibly.

Acceptable Use

  • Use the system for legitimate school guidance purposes only
  • Provide accurate information when booking or submitting inquiries
  • Do not submit abusive, harmful, or misleading content

Appointments

  • Arrive on time for scheduled sessions
  • Cancel or reschedule early when possible
  • Repeated no-shows may result in booking limits (school policy)

Availability

We aim to keep the system available, but access may be interrupted for maintenance or technical issues.

Not an Emergency Service

This platform is not an emergency hotline. If you need urgent help, contact school authorities or local emergency services.

Changes

These terms may be updated as policies or features change. Continued use indicates acceptance of updates.
