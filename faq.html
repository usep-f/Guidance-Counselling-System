FAQ

Common questions about appointments, inquiries, and confidentiality.

How do I book an appointment?
Open the Appointment feature, choose a slot, confirm details, then submit.
Can I send an inquiry instead of booking?
Yes, use the inquiry feature to send a confidential message to the Guidance Office.
Who can view my appointment or inquiry?
Only authorized guidance personnel (and required admins) can access counseling-related records.
How do I cancel or reschedule?
Use the system to cancel or reschedule as early as possible so others can book the slot.
Is this for emergencies?
No. For urgent help, contact school authorities or local emergency services immediately.
