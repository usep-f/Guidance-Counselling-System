Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account details (name, student ID, school email)
  • Appointment details (date/time, category, notes you provide)
  • Inquiry messages submitted through the platform
  • Basic technical logs (browser type, access logs) for security

How We Use Information

  • To schedule and manage guidance appointments
  • To respond to inquiries and provide student support
  • To improve system performance, reliability, and security

Confidentiality

Counseling-related information is handled confidentially and is accessible only to authorized guidance personnel and school administrators as required for guidance operations.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for counseling and school processes, or as required by school policy.

Security

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For questions about privacy, contact the Guidance Office using the details on the Contact section of the home page.
